Why It Matters MultiPUFFIN addresses the limitations of existing molecular foundation models by offering a domain - constrained multimodal approach that jointly learns related properties, improving the efficiency of molecular property prediction and enabling the prediction of thermodynamically meaningful properties. This work has implications for drug discovery, computational chemistry, and molecular machine learning.
